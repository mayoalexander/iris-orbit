The Moat

A 5-year head start, hidden in plain sight.

Our V6 async engine is store-and-forward orchestration — which is the deep-space comms standard (DTN Bundle Protocol v7). Iris Hive already dispatches jobs node-to-node across machines; a node on the Moon is architecturally identical. The physics is the blocker — never the software.

We ride the standards (BPv7 · LunaNet · CCSDS) instead of reinventing them → instant interoperability, near-zero data cost.
